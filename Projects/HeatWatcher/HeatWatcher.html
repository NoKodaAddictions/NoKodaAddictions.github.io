HeatWatcher Project - Device created to save children from dying of a heat stroke.


Intro

Many children are being left inside cars unknowingly and consequently dying due to an immense amount of heat.
In 2019, a whopping 51 cases were reported! Reason being that they don't think this kind of situation will happen to them

Current Solutions

Currently, There are 3 different solutions. Sensorsafe, General Motors Rear Seat Reminder Ssytem, And Driver's Little Helper.
Although these solutions are good, there are some limitations. With Sensorsafe, the costs for the booster seats are very expensive.
For General Motors, the system only works if the back door was opened and is only in select cars such as Chevorlet, Cadillac,
and GMC. Driver's Little helper on the other hand takes about 1 minute to send a notification to the driver. By the time it is sent,
the driver may be a few meters away (maybe in a grocery store, etc).

Criteria And Constraints

If I am going to create a device to solve this problem, this device needs to be:
1: Compact And Portable - Easier for drivers to place the module
2: Heat Resistant - To make sure the module doesn't short out from the rising heat.
3: Cost - Has to be cheap so many people can afford it

Solution

This is the current solution I created. It uses a motion sensor and a temperature sensor to detect people inside the car. Basically,
if the system detects a hot temperature, it will check to see if there is motion inside the car. If so, sound an alarm.

System Hardware And Connections:

1: Raspberry Pi 3
2: DHT22 Temperature/Humidity Sensor
3: PIR (Passive Infrared) Motion Sensor
4: Speaker
