RC Airplane Project

Intro

The RC Airplane Project is an attempt at creating a remote control aircraft with smart capabilites
The idea was first launched when Kevin from TheBackyardScientist made a glider that could be launched
from a potato cannon.

Credit: TheBackyardScientist

Designs and Prototypes

Prototype 3

This time, there was a groove created into to the middle of the wing.

This provided a much stronger connection with the fuselage

The glider lasted a lot longer, with 13+ flights.

After many flights, the fuselage started to crack, but the part connected to the fuselage was still on.

Prototype 2

The next prototype used the same airfoil from prototype 1.

This time, the fuselage was a balsa wood dowel. Like before, it was hot-glued on the wing.

Like before, the first 2 flights went well. The 3rd flight caused the fuselage to fall off the wing

The plane ended up stalling, and hitting the ground wing first.

Prototype 1

The first prototype was created similar to the design in Kevin's video.

It included the airfoil, and a plastic fuselage which was hot-glued to the wing.

For the first couple of flights, it went well. Then, the 3rd flight had failed.

The wing ended up hitting the ground first, and hard. This caused the fuselage to separate from the wing.
